Vienna by Billy Joel

All time fave! If you're one of those people who gets burnt out by so many responsibilities to do in life, then this song is perfect for you. It reminds us to slow down once in a while and that it's okay to rest too.

The Art of Getting By by Laura Zocca

This song is really comforting. It reminds us that someone out there understands us, and that even though everything feels tiring, there are still joys to life, so we must learn the art of getting by in order to survive.

Everglow by Coldplay

This song is very emotional. It depends on your interpretation, but the lyrics could be dedicated to your loved ones who passed away. There are a lot of emotions to feel in this song, but the grief and the long for someone are above it all. The lyrics are so relatable and impactful, so go and listen to it.

Glittery by Kacey Musgraves

If you love Christmas, then this is definitely for you! Far different than the moods of the previous songs, this song gives you the sweet Christmas feels. It's light, sweet, and it just makes you feel in love!

Mirrorball by Taylor Swift

This song is about constantly trying to be the best version of yourself for your loved one. I think many of us relate to this situation a lot. After all, it's natural to not want to disappoint the people around you. This song is very expressive. Go give it a try!

Written In The Stars by WENDY, John Legend

If you just want to feel light and in love, this should be on your playlist! It's about the constant reassurance that no matter how far each of you go, you're still going to be for each other because you were written in the stars. It's a song that drives out the hopeless romantic in you. Listen to it now!

Enough For You by Olivia Rodrigo

This song is about constantly trying to be enough for the person you love, but never being appreciated. This is also a very emotional song, so if you relate to it, then give it a go!

Shout About It by The Vamps

This song delivers to us intense emotions of love. It's about loving that person so much that you want to shout about it to the whole world. Definitely a great song!

Confetti by Tori Kelly

One of the best! This song serves as a reminder to celebrate and to enjoy even the littlest things in life! It also reminds us that life is too short to waste on trying to always be the best. It's a reminder that being at the top and the spotlight shouldn't be your only purpose. This is such a meaningful song!

The 1 by Taylor Swift

Last but not the least, of course, it's a song from Taylor! The 1 is a song that expresses disappointment that your relationship with the person who you thought was "the one" has ended. In the song, it said that it would've been fun, if you would've been the one. I think many of you could relate to this feeling. Go listen to it!
