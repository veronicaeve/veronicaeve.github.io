The Heirs

The Heirs is definitely one of the best k-dramas ever made! If you're a teen or even an adult who just wants to reminisce your highschool life, then this one will definitely be the k-drama for you! The drama revolves around the story of young heirs, heiresses and Cha Eun-Sa, a simple girl who finds herself entangled with the lives of completely different people. This drama really gives us the kilig feels, but more than that, it teaches us that even the richest, the prettiest, and the most privileged people have their own struggles too. All in all, the remarkable acting, plot, and the kilig feels it gave us will never be forgotten! Let's all pray for a season 2 :((

Scarlet Heart Ryeo

This drama puts us through an emotional rollercoaster :(( The plot revolves around a girl from the modern world who was suddenly brough to the Joseon era, where she tries to survive and to change history, but gets into a messy love triangle with two brothers fighting over the crown. The emotions felt in this drama are too deep, for it does not only revolve around love, but also family and friends. If you're into hard-core dramas, then you should watch this too!

Doctors

Another fave! This story revolves around a girl who was raised alone by her grandmother who later died due to malpractice. The death of her grandmother fueled her passion to become a surgeon because she wants to fight for her grandmother's death case. This story will never be forgotten, for it teaches us a lot about the struggles of people in order to be successful. It also revolves around the importance of family and how each family differs from each other. Some may seem ideal and perfect, while some are too broken to fix. All in all, this k-drama is very touching and deep. I recommend it to those who are really close to their grandmother and to those who want to be reminded that you can turn your dreams into reality.

It's Okay Not to Be Okay

Hands down to the best plot ever! This series is so extraordinary in such a way that no other k-drama could ever reach. It tackles mental health in a creative and sincere way, making its viewers feel and understand the emotions of each character in the story. This drama makes us feel complex emotions that we never though we would ever feel; thus, it really allows us to connect to each and every character of the story. I definitely recommend this to anyone who wants to understand more about mental health.

Descendants of the Sun

Last but not the least, who would forget the "Descendants of the Sun"?! The Song-Song couple starred in this drama and they didn't fail to wow us! The whole plot is adventurous, yet sad and lovely all at the same time. It really delivers the kilig feels to the audience, while also surprising us with twists and turns of the plot. 10/10, would recommend this to anyone!

K-MOVIE FAVORITES

1. Miracle in Cell No. 7

This movie had us crying! The whole plot is too sad and emotional, and it made us feel the pain and love that the father and his daughter felt. The best k-movie for me!

2. Train to Busan

This movie is one of the classics! It's so thrilling too! There's no boring moment in the movie. Even though it's zombie-themed, it still touches the viewers with the relationship of the father and his child.

3. Flu

Like the previous movie, this is also very thrilling and emotional. It just makes you feel hopeful and sad while watching it. The acting and the overall plot are good too!

4. Parasite

This movie is so shocking! The twists and turns, plus the parasitic lifestyle of the Kim family really makes the whole plot very interesting and thrilling. I also love the cinematography of this movie. Definitely recommend!
