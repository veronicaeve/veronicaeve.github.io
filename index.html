Fashion Tip!

Learn to balance proportions! I KNOW that you really want to mix and match a lot of tops and layers, but you should learn when to stop in order to avoid looking bulky! Sometimes, less is more. Take in mind your proportions and make sure that your outfits are flattering!

Beauty Tip!

Drop all the unnecessary products in your skincare routine! Those eyemasks and serums that you do not really need might just be costing you money! Again, sometimes, less is more. It's always best to consult your dermatologist first. You don't want all those unprescribed products to burn your face, that's a nightmare!
